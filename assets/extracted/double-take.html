> DOUBLE TAKE · SECOND-LOOK TERMINAL > STATUS: operational, careful, allergic to early verdicts > CASE FILES: 333 clues · 666 readings DOUBLE TAKE tests the first reading. The machine deals one clue: a pause, a receipt, a crop, a confession, a missing hour, a perfect alibi, a bad take, a role misunderstood. At first, the clue points somewhere. Then you read again. The same detail may become signal, noise, pressure, projection, category error, hidden constraint, or a sincere perspective seen from the wrong chair. The point is not to stop suspecting. The point is to stop worshipping the first explanation. Every card keeps the app simple: FIRST READ, SECOND READ, THE TEST. Nothing is saved to a server.
